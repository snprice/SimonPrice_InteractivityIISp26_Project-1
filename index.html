HOW BEING

AROMANTIC

AFFECTS MY LIFE

I am aromantic, which means that I don’t experience romantic attraction. I have no interest in romance or dating. In a world that heavily values romance and views it as a universal thing, it can be very isolating sometimes. People casually say things like “you know when you have a crush, blah blah blah,” or expect you to want to eventually date someone, and it can make me feel very out of place.

However, I think many people have the false impression that a life without romance is “sad” or “unfulfilled,” or that aromantic people are devoid of love, but that couldn’t be further from the truth! I am absolutely bursting with love for my friends and family, and I’ve never felt like I’m missing something without dating. Although it can make it hard to relate to others sometimes, I am very proud and happy being aromantic! :D
